<div><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>“BEHOLD, HE COMETH WITH CLOUDS”</strong></span></div>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Our God does not leave us in the dark concerning His future plans.&nbsp;“<em>Surely the Lord GOD will do nothing, but he revealeth his secret unto his&nbsp;servants the prophets</em>” (Amos 3:7). Our Lord Jesus said, “<em>But take ye heed:&nbsp;behold, I have foretold you all things</em>” (Mark 13:23). He that hath ears let&nbsp;him hear! Do not doubt His Word. Read. Hear. Take heed. For His Word&nbsp;will come to pass.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">To Daniel, a man greatly beloved of God, was given a vision which&nbsp;spanned some twenty-six centuries of future world history. Daniel saw&nbsp;the four major world powers of history portrayed by the four parts of a&nbsp;great image:</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Head of gold= Babylon 606-536 BC</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Breast and arms of silver = Medo-Persia 536-331 BC</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Belly and thighs of brass = Greece 331-168 BC</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Legs of iron = Rome 168 BC-AD 476</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Feet of iron and clay = (?) “the end time”</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">The last part of the great image is of debatable interpretation, but one&nbsp;school of prophecy sees in the feet of iron-clay mixture the end time&nbsp;Revived Roman Empire or the “United States of Europe.” According to&nbsp;this view, the world will come under the control of this Rome-dominated&nbsp;European power which will, at the return of Jesus Christ, be destroyed&nbsp;and superseded by the Kingdom of God.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">To the Apostle John, God gave a vision of that CLIMACTIC EVENT.&nbsp;“<em>Behold, he cometh with clouds</em>…” Who is He, but the great God and our&nbsp;Saviour Jesus Christ, Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end, the&nbsp;Creator and Almighty JUDGE to come. This MOMENTOUS EVENT&nbsp;which will bring to a conclusion world HISTORY is mentioned in l,800&nbsp;passages of the Bible. God has spoken, let the world take heed! The Lord&nbsp;of Creation is coming, soon! Are you ready? Ready to meet your Creator,&nbsp;the Judge?</span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>THOUGHT:</strong> Not lack of warning, but lack of faith.</span></div>
